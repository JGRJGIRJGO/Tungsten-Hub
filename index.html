SYSTEMS

Built around the decisions that matter in a run.

Lyra keeps the strategy state close to the game state: tower IDs stay stable, elevator selection is map-aware, and every replay carries its own actionable context.

Elevator handoff

Locates the matching lobby elevator, enters through its normal touch flow, and continues after the match teleport.

Loadout sync

Derives the exact towers placed in a strategy, clears the current loadout, equips the required five-slot set, and verifies it.

Ability capture

Records Call Of Arms, Mafia Call, Overcharge, and Chain COA rotations as strategy actions tied to their placed towers.

Replay logger

Streams every replay action into the always-on-top Lyra Autostrategies log, then exports the run when results arrive.

ACTION LOG

Every action stays visible while the strategy runs.

The in-game logger remains fixed above the game UI and records the precise replay sequence as it unfolds, from placement through final wave skip.
